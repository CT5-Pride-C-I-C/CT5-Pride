Remember

It's okay to take time for yourself. You deserve care and calmness. If you're experiencing ongoing anxiety or distress, please consider reaching out to the support resources we've provided, or speak with a healthcare professional.

Tips for Better Breathing

  • Practice regularly, even when you're not feeling anxious
  • Find a quiet, comfortable space where you won't be disturbed
  • Focus on the rhythm rather than forcing deep breaths
  • If your mind wanders, gently return attention to your breathing
  • Be patient with yourself - it takes time to build this skill
