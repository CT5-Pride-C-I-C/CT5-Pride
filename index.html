Welcome to CT5 Pride

Celebrating and supporting the LGBTQIA+ community in Whitstable and surrounding areas. Join us as we create a more inclusive, vibrant, and welcoming space for everyone.

Get Involved

Who We Are

Community

We're a grassroots organization dedicated to fostering a supportive and inclusive community for LGBTQIA+ individuals and allies in the CT5 area.

Celebration

We organize events, activities, and celebrations that bring our community together and raise visibility for LGBTQIA+ issues and achievements.

Support

We provide resources, advocacy, and a safe space for LGBTQIA+ people of all ages, backgrounds, and experiences in our local area.

What the Pride Colors Mean

Each color in the Pride flag has special significance, representing the diversity and strength of our community.

Red

Life and healing

Orange

Healing and comfort

Yellow

Sunlight and positivity

Green

Nature and growth

Blue

Peace and tranquility

Purple

Spirit and community
