About CT5 Pride

We're a grassroots community organization dedicated to supporting, celebrating, and advocating for LGBTQIA+ people in the CT5 area and beyond.

Our Mission

Celebrate

We organize events and activities that celebrate the diversity, achievements, and contributions of LGBTQIA+ people in our community.

Support

We provide a safe, welcoming space and resources for LGBTQIA+ individuals, families, and allies in the CT5 area.

Advocate

We work to promote equality, understanding, and acceptance while addressing issues that affect our community.

Our Values

Inclusion

We welcome people of all backgrounds, identities, and experiences. Our community is stronger when everyone feels valued and included.

Respect

We treat everyone with dignity and respect, creating spaces where people can be authentically themselves without fear of judgment.

Community

We believe in the power of community to create positive change, provide support, and celebrate our shared experiences.

Empowerment

We empower LGBTQIA+ people to live openly, pursue their goals, and make positive contributions to our broader community.
