About CT5 Pride

We're a community organization dedicated to celebrating and supporting LGBTQIA+ people in Whitstable and the surrounding areas.

Our Mission

Celebrate

Create joyful spaces and events that celebrate the diversity and vibrancy of our LGBTQIA+ community in the CT5 area.

Support

Provide resources, connections, and support to LGBTQIA+ individuals and their allies in our local community.

Connect

Build bridges between LGBTQIA+ people, local organizations, and the wider community to create lasting positive change.

Our Story

CT5 Pride was founded by local LGBTQIA+ residents who saw the need for a dedicated community organization in the Whitstable area. What started as informal meetups has grown into a vibrant community organization serving Whitstable, Tankerton, Seasalter, Swalecliffe, Chestfield, and Yorkletts.

Community-Led

We're run by and for the local LGBTQIA+ community, ensuring our work reflects real needs and aspirations.

Inclusive

We celebrate the full spectrum of LGBTQIA+ identities and experiences, creating spaces where everyone can be themselves.

Local Focus

Our deep connection to the CT5 area helps us create meaningful impact in our community.
