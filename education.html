Learning Paths

Follow these suggested learning paths to build your understanding of LGBTQ+ topics, whether you're new to the subject or looking to deepen your knowledge.

Beginner

Starting Point
1 Start with LGBTQ+ Terms (under Education) to understand basic terminology
2 Take the Pride Quiz to test your current knowledge

Intermediate

Building Knowledge
1 Dive deeper into LGBTQ+ History (under Education) for detailed understanding
2 Explore advanced terms and community-specific language
3 Challenge yourself with advanced quiz questions

Advanced

Deep Dive
1 Study historical movements and their impact through LGBTQ+ History
2 Understand intersectionality and current challenges
3 Share knowledge and support others in their learning
