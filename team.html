Meet the passionate individuals who make CT5 Pride possible. Our team is dedicated to creating an inclusive and vibrant LGBTQIA+ community across Whitstable and the entire CT5 postcode area, including Seasalter, Tankerton, Chestfield, Swalecliffe, and Yorkletts.

Our Leadership Team

CT5 Pride is led by passionate community members who volunteer their time and expertise to make our organisation thrive.

Freeman Dan Cope

Chair, Senior Director & Co-Founder

Dan Co-founded CT5 Pride to create the inclusive community space he wished had existed when he grew up in Whitstable. With a background in community organising, legal & technology, he leads our strategic vision.

Peter Simpson

Chief Operating Officer, Senior Director & Co-Founder

Peter brings years of operational experience to CT5 Pride, overseeing our day-to-day activities and ensuring our programmes run smoothly and effectively.
